Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoa
  • Maida
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya (or milk powder), flour, and milk to make a soft dough.
  2. Roll the dough into smooth, small balls.
  3. Fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  4. Soak them in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom or rose water.

Kalakand

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder

Recipe

  1. Mix crumbled paneer with sweetened condensed milk in a pan.
  2. Cook on low heat, stirring until it thickens and leaves the sides.
  3. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  4. Spread in a greased tray, let it set, then cut into pieces and enjoy!
